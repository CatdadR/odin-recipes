R's Nasi Goreng From Aussie SuperMarkets

Description:

Malay style Nasi goreng from ingreidents easily found in Australian supermarkets

Ingreidents

  • Day old rice
  • 500g diced raw chicken of choice
  • 2 - 3 Eggs
  • 1 - 2 Sliced large onions
  • 5 - 10 birds eye chilies
  • Sweet soy sauce to taste
  • Ayam brand nasi goreng paste
  • coconut oil

Steps

  1. Oil the wok
  2. Crack and add the three eggs. Cook on medium heat
  3. Add sliced onion and birds eye chilies, stirring them around with the eggs
  4. Once onion is soft add the diced chicken then stir until cooked
  5. Add the day old rice along with all of the nasi goreng paste and sweet soy
  6. Mix all ingreidents together until the rice is completly brown and broken up
  7. Turn off the gas and serve
  8. Enjoy!
